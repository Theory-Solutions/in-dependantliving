Everything you need

Designed for real life.
Not just emergencies.

In-Dependent Living covers the everyday — meds, schedules, activity, and safety — so nothing falls through the cracks.

💊

Medication Tracking

Log medications, set schedules, and track daily doses. Caregivers see what was taken and when — without needing to call.

📅

Shared Calendar with Privacy

Seniors keep full control. Mark events private so caregivers only see "Busy" — dignity preserved, coordination still easy.

🏃

Activity Monitoring

Syncs with Apple Watch and HealthKit. Steps, flights, heart rate — your family knows you're active without you reporting in.

🆘

One-Tap SOS

A single button to reach family or emergency services. Visible, simple, and always available right on the home screen.

🔗

Simple Pairing

Connect with family using a 6-character pairing code. Either side can generate or enter — no technical setup required.

📋

Caregiver Dashboard

A dedicated view showing today's meds, events, activity, and reminders — everything a caregiver needs at a glance.

Getting started
